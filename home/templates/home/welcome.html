About SORT Online

The Self-Assessment of Organisational Readiness for Research Tool (SORT) is a validated framework designed to help health and care organisations assess their readiness to engage in research. Originally developed through extensive consultation and research with nurses, midwives and allied healthcare professionals, health care organisations in identifying strengths, gaps, and development needs in their research culture and infrastructure.

SORT Online brings this tool to life in a streamlined, user-friendly digital platform.

You can then use the SORT Maturity Matrix—a framework to assess how developed each area is, from early-stage awareness to embedded practice—to understand your current position and plan next steps.
